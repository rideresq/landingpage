What If My Battery Is Dead?

If your car won't hold a charge after a jump, you likely need a new battery. Signs your battery is dying:

  • Battery is 3+ years old
  • You've needed multiple jumps recently
  • Car dies again shortly after jumping
  • Battery terminals are corroded

RideResQ can arrange a tow to a nearby auto shop if you need a battery replacement.
