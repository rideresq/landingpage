Frequently Asked Questions

What is RideResQ?

RideResQ is an AI-powered roadside assistance platform that connects stranded drivers in Denver to local towing companies and service providers without surge pricing or membership fees.

How is RideResQ different from AAA?

Unlike AAA, RideResQ has no annual membership fee — you only pay when you need help. Our AI provides transparent pricing upfront, so you know the cost before you commit.

Does RideResQ charge surge pricing?

No. RideResQ never charges surge pricing. We connect you directly with local providers at their standard rates.

How fast can I get help?

Average response time in the Denver Metro area is 15-25 minutes, depending on your location and provider availability.

How do I pay?

You pay the provider directly when service is complete. Most accept credit cards, debit cards, and cash.
