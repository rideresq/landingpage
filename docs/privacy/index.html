Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 17, 2026

RideResQ ("we", "us", or "our") operates the rideresq.com website and 1-844-789-RESQ phone service. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information.

Information We Collect

When you request roadside assistance, we collect:

  • Phone number
  • Name
  • Location (GPS or address you provide)
  • Vehicle information
  • Service request details

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Dispatch roadside assistance to your location
  • Send SMS notifications about your service request (confirmations, provider assignments, ETAs, arrival notifications)
  • Connect you with service providers
  • Improve our services

SMS/Text Messaging

By providing your phone number and requesting service, you consent to receive transactional SMS messages related to your roadside assistance request. Message frequency varies based on service status. Message and data rates may apply.

To opt out of SMS messages, reply STOP to any message. For help, reply HELP or call 1-844-789-RESQ.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

We share your information only with:

  • Service providers (tow companies, repair shops) necessary to fulfill your request
  • Service providers who assist our operations (SMS delivery, hosting)
  • Law enforcement when required by law

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide services and comply with legal obligations, typically no longer than 2 years after your last service request.

Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

Contact Us

Questions about this policy? Contact us at:

  • Email: privacy@rideresq.com
