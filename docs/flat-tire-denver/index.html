What To Do With a Flat Tire

  1. Pull over safely — Get off the road, away from traffic
  2. Turn on hazard lights — Make sure other drivers see you
  3. Stay in your car — Safer than standing on the roadside
  4. Call for help — RideResQ will send someone fast

Signs of a Flat Tire

  • Car pulling to one side
  • Bumpy, rough ride
  • Flapping or thumping sound
  • Tire pressure warning light
  • Visible damage or nail in tire
